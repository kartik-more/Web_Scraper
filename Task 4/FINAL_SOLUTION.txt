🔍 WHY FIELDS ARE "NA":

Field       Reason It's NA
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
discount    books.toscrape doesn't show discount percentages
reviews     books.toscrape doesn't show review counts on listing pages
mrp         books.toscrape doesn't show original prices (books don't have MRP)
sku         books.toscrape doesn't display SKU/UPC on listing pages

This is NOT a bug! It's the correct behavior. The scraper successfully extracts
whatever data IS available on the page.

For Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra - these fields WILL be filled!
